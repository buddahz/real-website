Risks and Challenges

There’s a security risk on our couriers and their cargo. Clients never have access to their location and the couriers will be able to report emergencies through their app window. Also in case a package isn’t deliverable by any reason, the client will be informed and it will be delivered in the nearest trade post to the receiving address.
Other than the real world problems there’s the need to keep the platform stable and safe and even if problems do appear, the platform should be able to keep working by not needing to be connected all the time to the main server to deliver the current packages.
